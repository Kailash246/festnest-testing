🎯 Our Mission

Democratizing Student Opportunities Across India

In India, students at tier-1 colleges have 10x more access to opportunities than students at tier-2 and tier-3 colleges — just because of geography and information asymmetry.

FestNest is built to fix this. By centralizing every campus event into one platform, we ensure that a student in Kakinada has the same access to opportunities as one in Mumbai.

We believe every talented student deserves a chance to shine — regardless of which college they attend.
